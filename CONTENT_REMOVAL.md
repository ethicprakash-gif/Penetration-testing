# Content Removal and Attribution

This project is an open-source learning resource. Along with my own work, it
collects and links to third-party material such as PDFs, documents, and notes
made by other people. That material belongs to whoever created it. It is shared
here in good faith, for education and reference only, and all rights stay with
the original owners.

## Found your content here?

If something here is yours and you want it credited differently or taken down,
just ask. There is no need to involve lawyers.

1. Open an issue on the GitHub repo, or reach me through https://m14r41.in.
2. Include a link to the material and a quick note confirming it is yours.
3. I will review it and remove or fix it as soon as I can.

I would always rather respect the original author than keep anything that causes
a problem. Thanks for understanding.
